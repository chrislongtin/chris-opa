Contractor Timesheet Management

Contractors have to account for the time spent on projects, OPA has the facility for Contractors to create timesheets and record on a day-to-day basis how much time they spent on projects. These timesheets are submitted to the administrator. The administrator reviews and approves or modifies the hours.   This will become a good basis for compensation since the Contractor setup allows the administrator to add the rate of compensation.

Once a Contractor submits a timesheet it cannot be modified by the contractor
