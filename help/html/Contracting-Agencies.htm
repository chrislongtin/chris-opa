Contracting Agencies

Contracting agencies are organizations that a placement agency has arrangemnts with whereby the placement agency undertakes to recruit candidates on behalf of the contracting agencies. Contracting agencies sub-contract the candidate search to the placement agency.

A contracting agencies also are organizations that supply contract personnel to an organization. 

 

An OPA coordinator can:

  • Add Contracting Agency
  • Edit/Delete Contracting Agency 

Steps:

Select Contracting Agencies from the menu bar.

To add an agency, select Add Contracting Agency. 

To edit or delete contracting agency information, select the appropriate link immediately beneath the agency you wish to modify.
