Edit/Delete CFP-Specific Appraisal Criteria

Appraisal criteria specific to a CFP can be changed when the CFP is being edited.

 

Steps:

 

1. Select Calls for Proposals from the menu, to view a list of all CFPs

2. Click on the Title of a CFP

3. A page will display all information about the CFP. 

4. Appraisal criteria specific to a CFP will be displayed. Click on Edit next to the criterion to modify , or delete  to remove the criterion.

 

All criteria from another CFP can also be imported.
