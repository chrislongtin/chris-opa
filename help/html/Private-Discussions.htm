Private Discussions

Public and private discussion forums can also be managed. The public discussion facility is a useful tool for policy discussions, for example. Private discussions are limited to a select group of people.

The functionalities for the Administrator/Coordinator are:

  • View Private Discussion
  • Add Private Discussion Documents
  • Remove Private Discussion Documents
  • Edit or Delete Discussion Forum Messages

The Public Website offer the public the means to participate in the discussion.
