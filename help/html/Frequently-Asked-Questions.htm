Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

The Public can view FAQ from the Public Website

Steps:

  1. Select Frequently Asked Questions from the menu bar
  2. Look for the question you want answered.
  3. You can also pose a question to the coordinator/administrator.

 

A coordinator/administrator can:

  • Compose FAQs
  • View FAQs

Steps:

  1. Select Communications Center from the menu bar
  2. Select View and Compose FAQs from the page. All existing FAQ will be displayed.
  3. To edit a FAQ, select the Edit link next to it
  4. To delete a FAQ, select the Delete button next to it.
  5. To compose a FAQ, enter the Question and the Answer on the displayed page and hit Submit button or select "Add FAQ" and then enter the Question and the Answer and hit Submit.
