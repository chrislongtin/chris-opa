Reviewer Timesheet Management

If Reviewers have to account for the time spent conducting reviews, OPA has the facility for Reviewers to create timesheets and record on a day-to-day basis how much time they spent on reviews. These timesheets are submitted to the administrator. The administrator reviews and approves or modifies the hours.  If Reviewers are paid for their services, this will become a good basis for compensation since the Reviewer setup allows the administrator to add the rate of compensation.

Once a Reviewer submits a timesheet it cannot be modified by the reviewer.
